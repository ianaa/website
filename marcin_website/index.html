About

I currently work on reinforcement learning and robotics at OpenAI. I used to work on gradient-based metalearning and learning algorithms at DeepMind. Before that I interned at Google (twice, including once at Google Brain) and Nvidia.

I finished a 4-year PhD program in 2 years at the University of Warsaw where I worked on cryptocurrencies (mostly Bitcoin) and leakage-resilient cryptography. Apart from MS in Computer Science I also studied Mathematics for 2 years and Physics for 1 year.

I also used to participate in various programming competitions. Among my most prominent awards are 3 gold medals at International Olympiad in Informatics.
